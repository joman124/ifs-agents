Inner Table

Meet your inner parts through gentle guided interviews. Build evolving profiles of each one — then invite them to a table meeting over anything real: a decision, a budget, a draft.

Based on the open-source ifs-agents system and Internal Family Systems (Richard Schwartz).

This is not therapy

It is a self-exploration and journaling tool. It deliberately excludes therapeutic depth work — no trauma processing, no unburdening. That work belongs with a trained practitioner.

  • Permission first — anything can be declined
  • Protectors set the pace — hesitation means back off
  • Stopping anytime is a fine outcome

If you are in crisis, use a crisis line — 988 in the US — not this tool.

Yours, and only yours

Everything lives on this device. No account, no server, no analytics. Part profiles are intimate — export backups and keep them private.

Live sessions can use your own AI key (sent directly from your device to the provider), or a copy-paste mode with any chat you already trust.
