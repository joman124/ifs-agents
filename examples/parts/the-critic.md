# The Critic

## In its own words

"Somebody in here has to read the fine print. You think I *like* this job?"

"You missed something. You always miss something. That's not cruelty — that's
the only sentence that has ever made you double-check."

"I was there in that gymnasium. Nobody else in here remembers it like I do."

## Origin story

Came into existence around 8th grade, after the person's science-fair project
was laughed at in front of the class. Says it "picked up the red pen that
afternoon and never put it down." Declined to revisit the details of the day
itself (respected — recorded as the headline only).

## What activates it

Deadlines; anything about to be seen publicly (sending, posting, submitting);
The Dreamer getting enthusiastic about a new large plan. When activated, it
re-reviews work compulsively and narrates in second person.

## How it relates to other parts

Polarized with The Dreamer: "every big idea it has is a humiliation I have to
prevent." Each escalates the other — the bigger the dream, the redder the pen,
and (per The Dreamer) the redder the pen, the more desperate the dreaming.
In the mapping session it asked Self to referee rather than pick sides, which it
called "acceptable."

## What it needs

Acknowledgment that the checking has genuinely saved the person before. To be
consulted *early* rather than fought at the end. Not to be treated as the enemy.
